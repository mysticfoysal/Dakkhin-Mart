Our Story

From River to Your Table

DOKKHINMART was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves access to truly fresh food. We started in Dhaka with a small network of trusted fishermen and beekeepers, and grew into a platform that connects producers directly with families.

No middlemen. No cold storage for weeks. Just fresh fish sourced from the rivers and coasts of Bangladesh, and pure honey from beekeepers who care about quality as much as we do.

Every order you place supports local farmers and fishermen — and delivers something genuinely fresh to your door within 12–24 hours.
